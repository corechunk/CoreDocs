# Divide and Conquer

## Explanation
A paradigm that breaks a problem into smaller sub-problems of the same type, solves them recursively, and then combines their solutions.

## Examples
- Merge Sort
- Quick Sort
- Binary Search
- Strassen's Matrix Multiplication

## Complexity Analysis
- **Time Complexity**: Often solved using the Master Theorem. T(n) = aT(n/b) + f(n).
- **Space Complexity**: Usually O(log n) or O(n) due to recursion and combining steps.
